Original Trilogy

In 1971, George Lucas began developing his own space opera.
And first Star Wars was released on May 25th, 1977.
He decided to make a Star Wars as series that would be a trilogy of trilogies.
The story of the original trilogy focuses on Luke Skywalker.

Prequel Trilogy

Prequel trilogy were developed during the outlining of he orignial two fims.
The story of the prequel trilogy focuses on the fall of the Galactic Republic.
And it also focuses on Anakin Skywalker turning into the dark side

Sequel Trilogy

Three trilogies of nine films.
The story of the sequel trilogy was focuses on rebuilding of the Republic, return of Luke.
It was focuses on the journey of the Rey and the following of footsteps of the Jedi.
