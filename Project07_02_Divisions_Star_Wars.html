General Armitage Hux is a fictional character in the Star Wars.
General Hux was first introduced in the 2015 film Star Wars: The Force Awakens.
He is a commander for the First Order leadership and able to exceeded only by Supreme Leader Snoke.
General Hux returned in the film's sequel, Star Wars: The Last Jedi.
